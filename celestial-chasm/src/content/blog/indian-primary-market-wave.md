## Capital Market Expansion Framework

India's primary stock issuance market is preparing for a major capital inflow cycle during the second half of 2026. Over **10 top-tier enterprises** are finishing their underwriting processes to initiate public equity listings on the national market platform.

### High-Profile Listing Pipeline:
1. **Jio Platforms:** Anticipated consumer network integration structure
2. **National Stock Exchange (NSE):** Market system operator listing
3. **SBI Mutual Fund:** Asset management capital offering
4. **Zepto & Acko:** Digital commerce infrastructure networks

This collection of public listings is projected to significantly expand aggregate domestic equity market capitalization, driven by steady monthly flows from systematic retail savings plans.
