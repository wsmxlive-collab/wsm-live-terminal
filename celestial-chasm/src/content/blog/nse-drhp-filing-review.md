## Exchange Infrastructure Audited Performance

The National Stock Exchange of India Limited (NSE) has officially submitted its Draft Red Herring Prospectus (DRHP) to market regulator SEBI, setting up a **100% Offer for Sale (OFS)** transaction architecture. The corporate listing target relies on an unlisted equity baseline valuation of **₹5 Lakh Crore**.

### Audited Financial Indicators (FY26 Audit):
* **Top-Line Compound Growth:** Revenue grew at a **20.51% CAGR** from FY22 to FY26
* **Consolidated Profit After Tax:** Reported at a record **₹10,302 Crore**
* **Audited Net Profit Margin:** **55.05%**, outperforming major international listed peers
* **Verified Return on Equity (ROE):** Tracking at an exceptional **32.98%**

Major selling stakeholders include State Bank of India, alongside institutional capital pools like the Canada Pension Plan Investment Board (CPPIB) and Morgan Stanley. This asset represents a highly competitive corporate infrastructure profile within global capital markets.
